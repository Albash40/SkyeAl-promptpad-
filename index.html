Prompt Notepad

Build structured prompts with our guided form. Craft perfect AI instructions with our step-by-step approach.

Give your prompt a descriptive title
Specify the role AI should assume Example: "Act as an experienced digital marketing specialist"
Clearly state what you want AI to do Example: "Create a comprehensive outline for a blog post about sustainable gardening"
Specify any limitations or rules AI must follow Example: "Do not use technical jargon. Keep it under 800 words. Include at least 3 practical tips."
Define how the output should be structured Example: "Provide the output as a markdown document with headings, subheadings, and bullet points"
